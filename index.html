About me

Hi, my name is Henrique Varela, I'm a 25 years old code enthusiast.
Organized, fast learner, easygoing and adaptable to every situation.

I'm portuguese and I've studied Landscape Architecture for about two and a half years, and since then im trying to get my life in order.
I started looking into music, coding, soldering and other DIY projects.
I'm self-taught at C#, C++, Html, Css and JavaScript. Started studying programming in Atec in June 2022.
